There are two different types of pads in football. Body pads and shoulder pads. Body pads are in the pants and your shirt. Shoulder pands are placed
on the shoulders and protects your shoulders and rib cage.

Shoulder pads:
Shoulder pads absorb some of the impact and shock through deformation. The padds are made so when you get hit, the shock
gets distributed through a larger pad that is made to regulate body temperature and prevent injury.

Shoulder pads have a hard plastic outer shell, like a helemt, but it has a soft shock absorbing foam underneath. The pads get secured to your body with straps.

Body pads:
For the lower half of your body, integrated football pants are a good way to go. These pants already have lightweight pads
sewed into them to protect the knees, thighs, hips and tailbone. The pads are made of EVA foams that help protect against collisions on the field.
If you don't have integrated football pants then a girdle also works just as well. There are different types of girdles. Some can have hip pads and tailbone pads
only and others can have all pads. It's like underwear, meaning you put it on and the pants over the girdle.

Deoending on the position you play, you might want to invest in extra padding around the ribs or back.
